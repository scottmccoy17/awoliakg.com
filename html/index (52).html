About AWOLIA KG

AWOLIA KG is a Muslim missionary school founded in 1996, and it continues to shape the next generation with dedicated teachers committed to providing quality education. We pride ourselves on teaching over 500 young students, nurturing their potential for a bright and successful future.

Our Places

AWOLIA KG provides various dedicated learning and recreational spaces to enhance your child's educational experience. Our facilities include well-equipped classrooms, safe and imaginative play zones, and a beautifully maintained compound, all designed to promote a vibrant atmosphere for learning and growth.

Our play zone encourages children to engage in physical activities, fostering teamwork and social skills.

Our classrooms are designed to be conducive to learning, featuring modern teaching tools to enhance the educational experience.

The full compound is a secure environment where children can explore various activities in a safe and engaging setting.

Service

At AWOLIA KG, we are dedicated to providing exceptional services that foster educational growth and development. Our team of qualified teachers is committed to nurturing and shaping every child's talents. We provide a range of programs, including after-school care, community workshops, and interactive learning initiatives, all designed to create a supportive and enriching environment for our young students.
